Ingredients

Item Quantity
Boneless Chicken Thighs 4 thighs
Basmati/Jasmine Rice 200g
Chicken Broth 250ml
Greek Yogurt 3/4th cup
Mayonnaise 1 cup
Spices: Smoked Paprika, Cumin, Turmeric, Black Pepper, Oregano, Salt, Sugar 1-2 tsp each
Bay Leaf 1 leaf
Onion 1/8th onion, finely diced
Garlic 5 Cloves
Lime 1 lime
White Vinegar 10g
Butter 1½ tbsp

Cooking instructions

Chicken

  1. Salt chicken thighs and leave to the side.
  2. Crush the garlic cloves into a paste.
  3. In a bowl, mix together ½ cup greek yogurt, ½ cup mayonnaise, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp black pepper, a sprinkle of oregano, the garlic paste, and juice of half a lime. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Thoroughly coat chicken thighs with the mixture in the bowl, then cover and let marinade anywhere from 1 hour to 1 day.
  5. After marinating, sear chicken thighs over medium-high heat for 6 to 7 minutes per side. Dice the chicken in the pan with a metal spatula 1 to 2 minutes before chicken is done cooking.

Yellow Rice

  1. In a pan over medium heat, melt 1 tbsp of butter.
  2. Once butter has melted, add in the diced onion, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp smoked paprika, ½ tsp cumin, and a bay leaf. Stir spices for about 30 seconds, careful to not let them burn.
  3. Add rice into the pan and toast with the spices for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken broth, turn up the heat, and bring broth to a boil. Once boiling, cover the rice and turn the heat down to the lowest setting and let the rice steam for about 20 minutes.
  5. Remove the rice and taste, add salt or butter to taste as needed.

White Sauce

  1. In a bowl, mix ½ cup mayonnaise, 1/4th cup greek yogurt, juice of 1/4th lime, 10g vinegar, ½ tsp black pepper, ½ tsp salt, and a sprinkle of smoked paprika and oregano. Mix well.
  2. Taste and add spices as needed, optionally pour into a squeeze bottle for easier use.

To serve, add chicken and rice to plate in equal parts, and optionally add shredded lettuce and diced tomato on the side along with a couple of pieces of pita bread. Add a generous amount of the white sauce on top and enjoy!
