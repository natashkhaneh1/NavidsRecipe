NY Food Cart Style Chicken and Rice

If you've been to New York and needed a bite to eat late at night, you may have come across one of the many food carts serving meat and rice platters. The meat and rice are heavily seasoned and taste amazing, and they usually come with their own white sauce or hot sauce as well which makes the dish taste even better than it already is. Unfortunately, it's hard to find this type of food in other places, but there's nothing stopping you from making something similar at home!
